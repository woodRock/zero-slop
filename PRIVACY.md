# Privacy Policy for ZeroSlop

**Last Updated:** March 12, 2026

ZeroSlop is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the ZeroSlop Chrome Extension.

## 1. Information We Collect
ZeroSlop is designed with a "privacy-first" approach. We do not collect any personal information, and we do not store any of your data on our own servers.

### 1.1. Data Stored Locally
The following information is stored **locally** on your device using your browser's secure storage:
*   **ZeroGPT API Key:** Provided by you to enable the AI detection service.
*   **Scan History:** A record of the tweets you have scanned and their detection scores (if you choose to keep it).
*   **Settings:** Your preferences, such as the "Auto-Scan" toggle.

### 1.2. Data in Transit
When you perform an AI detection scan, the following data is sent directly to the **ZeroGPT Business API** (api.zerogpt.com):
*   **Tweet Text:** The text content of the tweet you have selected to scan.
*   **API Key:** To authenticate your request with the ZeroGPT service.

This data is sent over a secure, encrypted connection (HTTPS). We do not intercept, store, or monitor this data.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information described above solely to:
*   Authenticate your requests with the ZeroGPT API.
*   Display AI detection scores for tweets directly in your Twitter/X feed.
*   Provide a seamless user experience across browser sessions.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
*   **No Third-Party Sharing:** We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third parties, except for the ZeroGPT API as required for the extension's core functionality.
*   **No Tracking:** ZeroSlop does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other methods to monitor your browsing activity across other websites.

## 4. Your Control and Data Deletion
You have full control over your data:
*   **Clear History:** You can delete your scan history at any time through the extension's popup menu.
*   **Remove API Key:** You can remove or update your ZeroGPT API key at any time.
*   **Uninstall:** Uninstalling the ZeroSlop extension will automatically remove all locally stored data from your browser.

## 5. Future Voluntary Features (Self-Reporting)
In future versions, ZeroSlop may include an optional feature to voluntarily report "false positives" or "false negatives" to help improve AI detection. This feature will be **strictly opt-in**, and no data will be shared without your explicit consent for each report.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the [ZeroSlop GitHub Repository](https://github.com/woodj/zero-slop).
